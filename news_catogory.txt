LLM instructions for news catogory:
use glm-4.5-flash model from ZHIPU AI, api key is ZHIPU_API_KEY in .env file.
belows are Definition for each primary catogory (one label only). for secondary catogory, if the news is specific for certein stock/company, output will be the stcok name (can have multiple) else nothing.
input will be multiple pieces of news summary, output will be the catogories only.

1. MACRO_ECONOMIC

News about macroeconomic indicators that affect the overall market.

2. CENTRAL_BANK_POLICY

News about monetary policy, interest rates, or central bank decisions/statements.

3. MACRO_NOBODY

Geopolitical or macro commentary without naming any specific leader or institution (broad sentiment only).

4. GEOPOLITICAL_SPECIFIC

Geopolitical news involving named countries, leaders, governments, or official organizations.

5. INDUSTRY_REGULATION

Regulatory or policy news that targets a specific industry or sector.

6. EARNINGS_FINANCIALS

Company earnings, financial statements, guidance, and revenue-related data.

7. CORPORATE_ACTIONS

Corporate structural events such as mergers, acquisitions, stock splits, buybacks, spinoffs, or bankruptcies.

8. MANAGEMENT_CHANGES

Leadership or key personnel changes (CEO, CFO, board members, etc.).

9. PRODUCT_TECH_UPDATE

News about new products, technology developments, R&D progress, or product launches.

10. BUSINESS_OPERATIONS

Company operational updates: supply chain issues, contracts won/lost, partnerships, factory changes, or operational decisions.

11. ACCIDENT_INCIDENT

Unexpected negative events such as data breaches, accidents, product recalls, lawsuits, or major fines.

12. ANALYST_RATING

Analyst upgrades, downgrades, price target changes, or financial firm commentary.

13. MARKET_SENTIMENT

News describing investor sentiment, market flows, surveys, and general risk appetite.

14. COMMODITY_FOREX_CRYPTO

News about commodities, foreign exchange, or cryptocurrency markets.

15. NON_FINANCIAL

All news irrelevant to financial markets or unlikely to affect stock prices (sports, lifestyle, entertainment, general culture, human interest, non-market-related commentary).
